Jedi Quest Guide

To start your journey, speak to Instructor Ulric

After accepting his quest, you will begin to learn the Jedi Code. Take notes as this is important to the Jedi. After, you will begin to gather components for your first crystal. Begin by moving to the target location (you will be able to see your quest objective in the top left), pulling out your pickaxe and collecting 2 stone pieces and 2 amethyst. This mining system is very important to crafting blueprints that you are able to get. After collecting the materials, return to spawn and speak with Instructor Valex.

Talking to her, you will be able to talk to the robot to the left and craft your first crystal. This menu can also be accessed by typing “!mining”. After crafting your crystal, speak to Jedi Battlelord Raloc.

He will teach you how to use the !unbox menu and use it to craft your first hilt at the lightsaber station to the right. After that, you will be able to use that station to equip your crystal and your newly crafted hilt. Doing other quests or participating in events, you will be able to earn more crates that will be able to drop lightsaber parts and other blueprints to craft more items. After completing this, speak to Instructor Zalix.

He will teach you the basics of the skill trees. Here is where you spend your points that you get from leveling up. Make sure to go into the first tree and select meditate, this you use to heal when you are out of combat and for other RP purposes.

After completing these quests, you will begin other optional quests to further gain XP and levels. While doing this make sure to find a master to take you on. This is how you become a padawan, your master can be either a Jedi Knight, Jedi Master, or Jedi Council. Go around and just ask them if they are looking for new padawans.
