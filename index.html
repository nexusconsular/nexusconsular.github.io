Welcome to the Nexus JvS Jedi Consulars

Jedi Consulars are a branch of the Jedi Order dedicated to the Force's spiritual and intellectual aspects. They serve as diplomats, healers, and scholars, using their deep connection to mediate conflicts, heal the injured, and study the mysteries of the Force. Preferring wisdom and peace over combat, they often work to maintain balance and harmony in the galaxy, whether through diplomacy, in-depth research, or supporting others with their healing abilities. Consulars embody the Order's commitment to knowledge and understanding, often guiding the Jedi and others with their insights.
